Hellman's Parmesan Crusted Chicken

The best at home chicken parmesan!!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Hellman's Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 4 tsp. italian seasoned dry bread crumbs

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Combine mayonnaise with cheese in a medium bowl
  3. Arrange chicken on baking sheet
  4. Evenly top with mayonnaise mixtures, then sprinkle with bread crumbs
  5. Bake until chicken is thoroughly cooked, about 20 minutes
