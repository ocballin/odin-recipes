Mary Potatoes

Cheesy potatoes to die for!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Frozen diced potatoes - Ore Ida, thawed
  • 8 tablespoons salted butter
  • 16 oz sour cream
  • 1 (10.5oz) can cream of mushroom soup
  • kosher salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 8 oz sharp cheddar - grated
  • 2 cups corn flakes - crushed

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Ft
  2. Spray a 9x13 baking dish with nonstick spray
  3. Melt the butter in a large bowl
  4. Whisk the sour cream and the soup into the melted butter until combined
  5. Add a big pinch of salt and pepper
  6. Fold in thawed potatoes - stir until completely incorporated
  7. Spread the mixture in the baing dish
  8. Top with grated cheddar
  9. Sprinkle the top with corn flakes
  10. Bake for 60 minutes
